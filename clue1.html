Sherlock Holmes: The Vanishing Manuscript

A priceless manuscript has been stolen from 221B Baker Street! Follow the clues to track down the thief.

Clue 1: Solve the following number puzzle:

You are given the following set of numbers:

7,7,8,15,5,3

Arrange the numbers in such a way that the sum of the first three numbers equals the sum of the last three numbers,

and the difference between the middle two numbers is equal to the sum of the first two.

What is the arrangement of numbers that satisfies these conditions?
